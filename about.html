About Me

I'm a dedicated Senior Software Engineer specializing in the Frappe Framework and ERPNext, with over 5 years of hands-on experience in building robust, customized Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solutions. My expertise lies in Python back-end development, full-stack Frappe app creation, client requirements gathering, and transforming complex business needs into functional systems across sectors like healthcare and real estate.

I pride myself on transforming raw business requirements into functional, scalable, and maintainable ERP systems. This involves everything from designing optimized database schemas (SQL/MariaDB) and streamlined approval workflows to creating performance-driven API integrations and providing guidance to junior developers. My career progression from Associate to Senior Engineer across multiple firms highlights my dedication to solving enterprise-level challenges efficiently.

I am passionate about creating effective system workflows, from initial conceptual design (like CRM flow in Visio) to final implementation, ensuring a seamless user experience (UI/UX).
